# 29_REPORT_TEMPLATE_PART2

## Detailed Assessment

This section defines the middle portion of the Founder Intelligence
Platform report.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Strengths

List the startup's strongest validated characteristics.

For each strength include:

-   Observation
-   Supporting evidence
-   Why it matters

Only include evidence provided by the founder.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Key Risks

Identify the most significant execution risks.

For each risk include:

-   Description
-   Potential impact
-   Evidence supporting the assessment

Do not speculate beyond the submitted information.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Evidence Gaps

Document missing information that prevents a higher-confidence
assessment.

Examples include:

-   Customer validation
-   Revenue evidence
-   Market sizing
-   Unit economics
-   Competitive positioning

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Priority Actions

Rank the highest-impact actions from highest to lowest priority.

Each action should include:

-   Objective
-   Expected outcome
-   Success indicator

Recommendations should be practical, measurable and focused on reducing
uncertainty.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

End of Part 2.
