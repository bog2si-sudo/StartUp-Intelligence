# 29_REPORT_TEMPLATE_PART1

## Report Structure

This document defines the first section of the Founder Intelligence
Platform report template.

The template is the production output consumed by users after completing
an assessment.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Cover

-   Founder Intelligence Platform
-   Startup Readiness Assessment
-   Assessment Date
-   Report Version
-   Assessment ID

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Executive Summary

Provide a concise summary including:

-   Overall readiness
-   Primary strengths
-   Highest execution risks
-   Recommended immediate focus

Maximum length: 250 words.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Overall Readiness Score

Display:

-   Overall score (0--100)
-   Readiness category
-   Confidence level

Suggested readiness categories:

-   Early Validation
-   Emerging Readiness
-   Investor Preparation
-   Growth Ready

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Dimension Overview

Include scores for:

-   Team
-   Market
-   Product
-   Traction
-   Business Model
-   Execution

Each dimension must include a one-paragraph explanation grounded in the
founder's responses.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

End of Part 1.
