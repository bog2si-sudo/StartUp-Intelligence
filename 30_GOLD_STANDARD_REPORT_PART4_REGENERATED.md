# Priority Actions

## Priority 1 --- Validate Customer Demand

Complete structured customer interviews focused on confirming the
problem, willingness to adopt the solution and purchase criteria.

**Success Indicators**

-   At least 20 interviews completed
-   Consistent problem patterns identified
-   Clear purchase signals documented

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Priority 2 --- Validate Pricing

Test pricing assumptions before increasing investment in product or
marketing.

**Success Indicators**

-   Pricing conversations completed
-   Objections documented
-   Pricing hypothesis refined

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Priority 3 --- Build Repeatable Acquisition

Focus on one primary acquisition channel and measure its repeatability
before expanding.

**Success Indicators**

-   Customer acquisition cost measured
-   Conversion funnel established
-   Repeatable acquisition process demonstrated

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Decision Readiness

Based on the available evidence, additional validation should precede
significant commitments such as fundraising, major hiring or accelerated
market expansion.

Future capital allocation decisions should be supported by measurable
commercial evidence rather than assumptions.
