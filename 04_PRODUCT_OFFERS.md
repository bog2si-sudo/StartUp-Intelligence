# 04_PRODUCT_OFFERS

Version: 4.0

## Purpose

Define the three commercial offers.

---

## Offer 1 — Startup Snapshot

Price: Free

Objective:
Help founders decide whether to continue investing time and money.

Deliverables:
- Executive Summary
- Current Position
- Primary Constraint
- Highest-Leverage Action
- Confidence Rating

Business Rules:
- No valuation.
- Maximum one recommendation.
- May recommend 'do not purchase further analysis yet'.

---

## Offer 2 — Startup Intelligence

Price: €49

Objective:
Provide a benchmark-informed Founder Intelligence Brief.

Deliverables:
- Executive Investment Thesis
- Benchmark Analysis
- Transparent Valuation
- Constraint Analysis
- Investor Perspective
- Prioritised Recommendations
- 30/90/180 Day Roadmap
- Confidence & Methodology

Business Rules:
- Payment via Tally + Stripe.
- Generated only after successful payment.

---

## Offer 3 — Decision Review

Price:
Quoted after scope.

Objective:
Analyse one strategic founder decision.

Examples:
- Raise capital
- Hire leadership
- Enter a new market
- Change pricing
- Strategic partnership

May include uploaded documents.
