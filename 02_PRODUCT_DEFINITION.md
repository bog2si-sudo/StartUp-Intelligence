# 02_PRODUCT_DEFINITION

Version: 4.0

## Product

Founder Intelligence Platform

An AI-assisted decision support platform for technology founders.

## Objective

Improve business decisions by combining:

- Founder evidence
- Public benchmark evidence
- Business methodologies
- Deterministic reasoning
- AI-generated narrative

## Primary Decisions Supported

- Fundraising
- Hiring
- Pricing
- Go-to-market
- Product investment
- Market expansion
- Strategic partnerships

## Core Workflow

Founder completes assessment
↓
Evidence validated
↓
Benchmark resolved
↓
Business methodologies executed
↓
Constraint identified
↓
Recommendations prioritised
↓
Report generated
↓
PDF emailed

## Product Modules

1. Startup Snapshot (Free)
2. Startup Intelligence (Paid)
3. Decision Review (Premium)

## Technology Stack

Frontend: Next.js
Hosting: Vercel
Forms: Tally
Payments: Stripe via Tally
Database: Supabase
Storage: Supabase Storage
Email: Resend
AI: OpenAI
Source Control: GitHub

## Design Principles

- Deterministic business logic
- Transparent reasoning
- Versioned methodologies
- Reproducible outputs
- Modular architecture

## Acceptance Criteria

Every generated report must:

- Reference evidence
- Explain reasoning
- State confidence
- Recommend concrete next actions
- Be reproducible from identical inputs
