# 11_SCORING

Version: 4.0

## Purpose

Summarise available evidence.

Scores support reasoning.
They never replace reasoning.

## Categories

- Market
- Product
- Commercial
- Team
- Execution
- Fundraising Readiness

Scale: 0–100

## Rules

- Deterministic
- Versioned
- Reproducible

Missing information lowers confidence, not the score.

Every score must include:
- Evidence used
- Explanation
- Confidence
