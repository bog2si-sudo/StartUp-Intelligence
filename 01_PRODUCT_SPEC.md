# 01 — Product Spec

## Product Name

Recommended working name:

**Startup Readiness & Capital Allocation Check**

Alternative names:

- Startup Decision Check
- Startup Readiness Report
- Next-Stage Readiness Review

## Positioning

A practical, benchmark-informed startup assessment for founders in Romania and emerging European ecosystems.

It helps founders understand what is missing, what matters most, and where their next money and effort should go.

It does not provide a formal valuation, investment advice, fundraising advice, legal advice, tax advice, or accounting advice.

## Target Market

Primary target:

Founders of technology-enabled or technology-led startups in Romania and emerging European ecosystems.

Geographic focus:

- Romania
- Moldova
- Bulgaria
- Serbia
- Croatia
- Slovenia
- Slovakia
- Czechia
- Hungary
- Poland
- Baltic countries
- Ukraine when appropriate
- Other emerging European markets case by case

No US focus.

The system may use US or mature-market data only as a last-resort proxy if no closer benchmark exists. If this happens, confidence must be reduced and clearly explained.

## Ideal users

The best users are founders who are asking one of these questions:

- Should I build more or validate more?
- Should I spend money on development, sales, marketing, hiring, or fundraising?
- What is preventing us from reaching the next stage?
- Is our startup credible enough for investors, partners, or internal stakeholders?
- What evidence do we need to strengthen the story?
- What would make our valuation logic more believable?
- What should we do in the next 30–90 days?

## Non-ideal users

Do not over-serve:

- pure idea-stage founders with no clear problem or customer,
- people looking for guaranteed investor introductions,
- founders looking for formal valuation,
- founders needing legal, tax, regulated financial, accounting, or securities advice,
- deep technical architecture reviews,
- regulated industries requiring specialist domain expertise unless explicitly scoped.

## Product Ladder

### Offer 1 — Free Decision Check

Price: Free  
Time: 3–5 minutes  
Questions: 14–16  
Purpose: identify the main constraint and whether deeper analysis is worth paying for.

Question answered:

> Am I ready to spend more money, and if yes, where would it matter most?

Output:

- readiness score,
- main constraint,
- evidence quality,
- one investor-style objection,
- one recommended experiment,
- deeper-analysis recommendation: yes / not yet / maybe.

Important: this offer must sometimes say "not yet".

### Offer 2 — Startup Readiness & Capital Allocation Report

Recommended price: €49  
Launch discount: €39 if needed  
Time: 7–10 minutes  
Questions: 26–30  
Purpose: provide a structured, benchmark-informed report with evidence gaps, valuation context, and prioritised action plan.

Question answered:

> What should I improve next to increase readiness, credibility, and valuation logic?

Output:

- expanded executive summary,
- readiness score,
- evidence map,
- team readiness,
- traction and market signals,
- benchmark pattern comparison,
- valuation methods,
- weighted directional valuation range,
- investor objection map,
- capital allocation priorities,
- what must become true to reach the next stage,
- confidence and data coverage,
- disclaimer.

### Offer 3 — Next-Stage Readiness Review

Price: not shown as fixed public price  
Flow: request form, then scoped recommendation  
Purpose: help founders with a specific maturity-relevant next-stage decision.

Question answered:

> What exactly is missing to reach the next stage, and how should I close that gap?

This offer is not for everybody.

Entry criteria:

The founder should have at least some of:

- a concrete product or MVP,
- a defined target customer,
- a committed founding team,
- some customer validation,
- users, pilots, customers, or revenue,
- a concrete decision to make in the next few months.

If none are present, route to Free Decision Check or Paid Report.

Scope focus:

- closing the next-stage gap,
- capital allocation,
- narrative vs evidence,
- execution priorities,
- fundraising readiness,
- pitch deck and numbers consistency,
- 30–90 day roadmap.

Explicit exclusions:

- standard investor matchmaking,
- legal or tax advice,
- formal valuation,
- due diligence,
- accounting,
- regulated financial advice,
- deep specialist technical review.

Investor targeting may exist only as case-by-case advisory, not core promise.

## Pricing Logic

### Free

Free because it generates trust and leads.

### Paid Report

€49 is recommended.

Reason:

- high enough to avoid toy perception,
- low enough to feel like a deal,
- reasonable for founders in emerging European ecosystems,
- leaves room for €39 discount,
- does not require human delivery cost.

### Next-Stage Review

Do not publish a single fixed price.

Use this public explanation:

> Every company reaches its next stage differently. Some founders need a second opinion on a pitch deck. Others need help deciding how to allocate six months of runway. After you submit your request, you receive a recommended scope, whether AI-only or human input is appropriate, expected turnaround, and a fixed price before any work begins.

Internal pricing bands may exist, but not displayed publicly:

- AI-only document-assisted review: low fixed price
- AI + human review: medium fixed price
- advisory / workshop / execution support: custom

## Product Boundaries

Always say:

- directional,
- benchmark-informed,
- confidence-rated,
- based on available evidence,
- not formal valuation.

Never say:

- correct valuation,
- guaranteed valuation,
- investor-ready guarantee,
- industry average unless statistically true,
- full market coverage,
- all comparables.
