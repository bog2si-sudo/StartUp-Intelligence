# 12_CONFIDENCE

Version: 4.0

## Purpose

Measure certainty of conclusions.

## Confidence Areas

- Founder Evidence
- Benchmark Match
- Valuation
- Recommendations
- Overall Assessment

Levels

- High
- Medium
- Low
- Insufficient

## Factors

- Completeness
- Consistency
- Benchmark quality
- Method suitability
- Data availability

## Rules

Every major conclusion displays its confidence level together with a short explanation.

Confidence never represents startup quality.
