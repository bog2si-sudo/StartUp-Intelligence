# 15_REASONING_ENGINE

Version: 4.0

## Purpose

Convert evidence into prioritised founder decisions.

## Pipeline

Founder Evidence
→ Benchmark Evidence
→ Business Methodologies
→ Constraint Identification
→ Recommendation Engine
→ Prompt Builder
→ Founder Intelligence Brief

## Core Rules

- One primary constraint.
- Maximum two secondary constraints.
- Recommendations derive from evidence, never directly from the LLM.
- Every conclusion must be reproducible.

## Outputs

- Constraint
- Recommendations
- Confidence
- Reasoning summary
