# Evidence Gaps

## Customer Validation

Customer validation is progressing but remains insufficient to support
higher-confidence commercial decisions.

Recommended evidence:

-   Additional customer interviews
-   Structured feedback analysis
-   Validation across multiple customer segments

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Pricing Validation

Pricing assumptions have not yet been supported by sufficient market
evidence.

Recommended evidence:

-   Pricing experiments
-   Willingness-to-pay interviews
-   Commercial conversion data

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Commercial Metrics

Strengthen operational evidence by tracking:

-   Customer acquisition cost
-   Conversion rate
-   Retention
-   Expansion revenue
-   Lifetime value

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Benchmark Context

Comparable benchmark patterns indicate that startups progressing to the
next maturity stage typically demonstrate stronger customer validation,
repeatable acquisition and more consistent commercial evidence.

Benchmark comparisons are directional rather than prescriptive.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Overall Interpretation

The business shows encouraging fundamentals but should continue
prioritising evidence generation before materially increasing
operational expenditure or pursuing external investment.

Future assessments should measure progress against the identified
evidence gaps.
