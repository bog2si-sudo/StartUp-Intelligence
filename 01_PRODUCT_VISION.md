# 01_PRODUCT_VISION

Version: 4.0

## Vision

Build the most trusted founder decision-support platform for early-stage technology companies in Romania and Central & Eastern Europe.

## Mission

Help founders allocate limited time, capital and attention toward the actions most likely to create enterprise value.

## Core Principles

1. Evidence before opinion.
2. Transparency before complexity.
3. Explain every important conclusion.
4. AI communicates; business logic decides.
5. Recommendations compete for scarce founder resources.

## Success

A successful assessment changes a founder's next decision.

## Non-goals

- Predict startup success.
- Guarantee valuation.
- Replace legal, financial or tax professionals.
- Replace founder judgement.

## Guiding Question

Will this feature help founders make better decisions?

If not, it should not be built.
