# 03_TARGET_CUSTOMER

Version: 4.0

## Primary Audience

Technology-led and technology-enabled founders in:

- Romania
- Central & Eastern Europe

## Company Profile

- MVP or early commercial product
- Early customer validation
- Seeking growth, funding or strategic clarity
- Limited resources requiring prioritisation

## Typical Questions

- Should I raise now?
- What is holding valuation back?
- Where should I invest next?
- Which evidence is missing?
- What would investors likely ask?

## Not Target Customers

- Large enterprises
- Public companies
- Lifestyle businesses
- Traditional SMEs with no growth ambitions

## Success Measure

The founder leaves with one clearer, evidence-supported decision.
