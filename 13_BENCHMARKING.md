# 13_BENCHMARKING

Version: 4.0

## Purpose

Provide external market context using curated public benchmark data.

## Matching Priority

1. Romania
2. Central & Eastern Europe
3. European Union
4. Closest valid proxy

## Public Fields

- Country
- Sector
- Business model
- Stage
- Funding round
- Funding amount
- Public valuation (when available)
- Investors
- Founders
- Employees
- Company age

## Rules

- Never invent benchmark metrics.
- Clearly distinguish observed, estimated and unavailable data.
- Every benchmark includes a confidence rating and selection rationale.
