# 07_DATA_MODEL

Version: 4.0

## Purpose

Define the business data used throughout the platform.

## Evidence Types

### Founder Evidence
- Assessment responses
- Company profile
- Objectives
- Constraints
- Uploaded documents

### Benchmark Evidence
- Country
- Sector
- Stage
- Funding
- Valuation (public)
- Investors
- Employees
- Company age

### Derived Evidence
- Scores
- Confidence
- Valuation outputs
- Constraints
- Recommendations

### Generated Outputs
- Founder Intelligence Brief
- PDF
- Email

## Rule

Derived data is always reproducible from founder evidence, benchmark evidence and methodology versions.
