# 10_DECISION_REVIEW

Version: 4.0

## Purpose

Evaluate one specific founder decision using existing assessment data and optional uploaded documents.

## Supported Decisions

- Fundraising
- Hiring
- Pricing
- Market expansion
- Strategic partnership
- Product investment
- Organisational change

## Inputs

- Previous assessment
- Decision description
- Supporting documents
- Desired outcome
- Constraints

## Outputs

- Decision summary
- Evidence supporting the decision
- Evidence against the decision
- Alternatives
- Recommended course of action
- Confidence

## Acceptance Criteria

Exactly one recommended path is presented.
